About Me

Hello! My name is João Pedro Rosa Anacleto, and I am a university student passionate about the field of Computer Engineering.

At 20 years old, I was born in Barreiro and currently live in Montijo.

Throughout my academic journey, I have had the opportunity to gain relevant professional experience, which has allowed me to develop technical skills in various programming languages such as Java, Python, C, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Additionally, I have knowledge of frameworks like Django and database systems like MySQL.

I have always sought to expand my horizons through diverse interests and hobbies, including playing football, going to the gym, reading magazines related to my field, watching series, playing video games, among others.

Regarding languages, I am fluent in Portuguese (native), with intermediate knowledge of Spanish and English, which gives me an advantage in global communication.

Throughout my university path, I have participated in projects aimed at expanding our knowledge in specific subjects, which has helped shape my vision and career goals.

I highly value integrity and collaboration, as I believe they are essential pillars for success in any endeavor.

Furthermore, I constantly strive for professional excellence, seeking to improve my skills and knowledge in the field of Computer Engineering.

I am thrilled to share my work and passion through this digital portfolio.

Thank you for visiting the website, and I hope you enjoy it! 🖒
